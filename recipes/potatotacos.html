Potato Tacos

Soft Potatoes + Soft Tacos = Match made in Heaven!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Potatoes
  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Pepper
  • 1/4 Tsp Cayenne

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into small bite-sized pieces. Place in a bowl and cover with water--let sit 30 minutes and then strain, rinse and gently pat dry.
  2. To a large pan over medium heat, add the olive oil, the potatoes and the spices. Mix well and cook, stirring often, until golden brown on most sides--this may take some time but it's worth it!
  3. Serve on tortillas with toppings of choice!
